About Me

I'm Quinto Bransen — a media and social strategy professional with a decade of real-world experience helping brands connect with their audiences in meaningful, lasting ways. Grounded in a rigorous academic foundation from New York University, I blend analytical precision with creative instinct.

After leading social media strategy at Dima Solutions for over six years, I transitioned in 2019 to full-time remote freelancing — offering focused, high-impact services to clients across the globe through Upwork, Fiverr, and PeoplePerHour.

Whether growing a brand's social presence, organizing financial records, or revamping a client's professional identity — I deliver with precision, professionalism, and purpose.
